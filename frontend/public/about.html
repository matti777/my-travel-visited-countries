About

Welcome

Countries of Earth is a free place to remember the countries you’ve been to. Think of it as a friendly travel journal for your adventures around the world.

Explore your travels

Browse the places you’ve visited by name or by continent, see them on a map, follow them along a timeline, or check how much of the world you’ve covered so far.

Make each visit yours

Add the date you were there, a few notes, tags that help you find trips later, and links to photos or videos from the journey.

Share with friends

Send a link so friends can see your list of countries. You can also keep a list of friends’ travel pages and peek at where they’ve been.
